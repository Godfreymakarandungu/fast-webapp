Farm Sustainability Tool

Prototype of the Farm Sustainability Tool for nutrients (FaST), in line with requirements of Article 12, and Annex III, of the post-2020 Common Agricultural Policy Strategic Plans proposal.

Design principles:

  • Optimized for use on small farms;
  • Integrates economic and environment/climate legal constraints;
  • Proposes future-proof digital solutions for simplifying farm and CAP-related tasks;
  • Provides access to a large user base of small farmers to propose advanced services to (public and private);
  • Makes full use of EU-developed remote sensing (Copernicus), geo-positioning (Galileo), cloud-based data and information access capabilities (e.g. DIAS).

The proposed FaST platform:

  • A modular architecture that responds to a large volume of users, takes into account the evolving nature of the tool (new modules and applications) and integrates additional services.
  • A multi-technology architecture that supports the adoption and implementation of the tool and provides adaptability to existing skills, know-how and codebase.
  • A cloud-native architecture that ensures a consistent cloud strategy.

Developed as part of the European Commission support for the implementation of the Farm Sustainability tool for Nutrients (FaST).

All the code produced for the application and the platform has been published on public repositories, available here.
